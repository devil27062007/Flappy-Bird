# Flappy Bird - Shop Edition

A Flappy Bird clone built with **HTML5 Canvas** , **CSS** and **JavaScript** - featuring an in-game shop , power-ups and a currency system to keep things interesting.

---

## how to play

| Control | Action |
|--------|--------|
| `Click` or `Spacebar` | Flap wings |
| Click skill slots (top-left) | Activate a power-up |

**Objective:** Fly through the pipes , rack up points and use your power-ups wisely to surviving as long as possible.

---

## Currency

Every point you score is worth **3 coins**.Spend them in the shop to stock up on power-ups before your next run .

---

## shop

Open the shop from the main meu to browse and purchase power-ups using your earned coins.

---

## Power-ups

| Power-up | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **Shield** | Absorbs one hit and gives you a second chance |
| **Rocket** | Destroys all incomming missiles |
| **Invisibility** | Makes you invisible for 3 seconds - score multiplier x5 during this time |
| **Invincible** | Makes you invincible for 5 second you dont die in that even hit by missile or hit on pippes |
| **Gravity** | Slows down vertical movement .It helps t0 cross pipes easily |

>Power-ups can be stacked for combined effects.
